URL Shortener Microservice

How to use:

  • Use the form provided to create a shortened url; or
  • Send a POST request with key-value: "url: https://website.com"

NOTE: Your url must begin with https:// or http://

Example Short Url:

[url]/api/shorturl/1

will be redirected to:

https://twitter.com/
